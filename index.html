What is the Law of Vibration?

Everything in the universe has a vibration. The objects we see around us are constantly in motion, vibrating and traveling at a specific frequency.

This same principle also applies to us. Our thoughts, feelings, desires create our vibration. Our vibration determines the frequency that we send out into the universe. When one learns to adjust their vibration, they will also be able to attract abundance and transform their lives in ways that they never thought were imaginable.

To utilize the Law of Vibration, it is important to learn to shift your paradigms. Shifting your paradigms requires a shift in your thoughts. By changing your thoughts, you can manifest things that you always wanted to attract. In short, your state of mind is what creates your reality. It can be hard to understand the concept of the Law of Vibration because it is not something that we can touch, smell, hear or feel with our five senses. At Mind Paradigm, we work with you to achieve your wants and goals by teaching you ways to shift your frequency and paradigm. Our coaching sessions have been specifically designed to provide frequent, consistent mentoring that will help shape and solidify your new paradigm, and transform your life.
